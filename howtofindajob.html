How to Find a Job

Tips and resources for job seekers in Lithuania

← Back to Work

Job Hunting Tips

  • Update your CV: Use a modern template and highlight relevant skills and accomplishments.
  • Create a strong LinkedIn profile: Include a professional photo, detailed experience, and recommendations.
  • Tailor your cover letters: Personalize each letter for the job and company you’re applying to.
  • Use local job platforms: Many employers post on Lithuanian-specific sites first.
  • Network: Attend events, seminars, or meetups. Referrals matter!
  • Learn Lithuanian: While not always necessary, even basic language skills can increase your chances.

Popular Job Portals

  • CVbankas.lt – One of the largest job boards in Lithuania.
  • CVonline.lt – Well-established platform with various listings.
  • Work in Lithuania – A government-supported site for international talent.
  • LinkedIn – Great for professional networking and job searching.
  • Lithuanian Employment Service (UŽT) – State-supported services for both locals and foreigners.

Where to Look for Jobs

Jobs in Lithuania can be found in various fields, especially:

  • Information Technology and Engineering
  • Customer Service and Shared Service Centers
  • Finance, Accounting, and Fintech
  • Manufacturing and Logistics
  • Startups (especially in Vilnius and Kaunas)

Preparing for Interviews

  • Research the company beforehand and understand its values and services.
  • Practice common interview questions in English and, if possible, Lithuanian.
  • Dress professionally – even for online interviews.
  • Ask questions about team culture, expectations, and next steps.

Documents You Might Need

  • Updated CV and Cover Letter
  • Degree certificates and translations (if applicable)
  • Reference letters from previous employers
  • Work permit (if you're a non-EU citizen – check Work Permits page)

Additional Resources

  • Europass CV Tool – Create a CV in European format.
  • Invest Lithuania – See which international companies are operating in Lithuania.
  • Startup Lithuania – For those interested in startups or founding their own business.
