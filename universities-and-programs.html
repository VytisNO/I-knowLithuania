Top Universities in Lithuania

  • Vilnius University (VU) – Established in 1579, one of the oldest universities in Eastern Europe. Offers programs in sciences, humanities, law, business, and more.
  • Kaunas University of Technology (KTU) – Specializes in engineering, IT, and innovation-oriented business programs.
  • Lithuanian University of Health Sciences (LSMU) – Leading medical and veterinary sciences university in the Baltics.
  • Vytautas Magnus University (VMU) – Liberal arts-focused with strong international exchange programs.
  • Vilnius Tech – Known for architecture, engineering, and aviation programs.

Programs in English

Many universities in Lithuania offer full-time Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD programs in English. Popular fields include:

  • Information Technology (Cybersecurity, Software Engineering, Data Science)
  • Business & Management (International Business, Digital Marketing, Finance)
  • Health Sciences (Medicine, Public Health, Nursing, Pharmacy)
  • Social Sciences (Psychology, Sociology, International Relations)
  • Creative Arts & Design (Multimedia Design, Visual Arts, Architecture)

Study Duration

  • Bachelor’s degree: 3–4 years
  • Master’s degree: 1.5–2 years
  • PhD studies: 3–4 years

Some fields, like medicine, may take 5–6 years for integrated studies.

Internships and Practice

Many programs include internships as part of the curriculum. Universities have partnerships with Lithuanian and international companies, allowing students to gain real-world experience.

Scholarships and Funding

Students can apply for:

  • Government-funded scholarships (especially for non-EU students)
  • University-specific tuition waivers or merit-based scholarships
  • Study in Lithuania Scholarship Opportunities

How to Explore Programs

Use the following platforms to compare and apply:

  • Study in Lithuania – Official Portal
  • Apply to Study in Lithuania
  • University websites (each lists full program catalogues and tuition details)
