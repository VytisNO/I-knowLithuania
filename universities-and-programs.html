Top Universities in Lithuania

  • Vilnius University – One of the oldest universities in Eastern Europe, offering a broad range of programs.
  • Kaunas University of Technology – Focused on engineering, IT, and business programs.
  • Lithuanian University of Health Sciences – Ideal for students interested in medicine and health sciences.

Programs in English

Most major universities offer Bachelor's and Master's programs in English in fields such as:

  • Business and Management
  • Medicine and Health Sciences
  • Political Science and International Relations
