Everyday Life

Lithuania offers a high quality of life, clean environment, and modern infrastructure. Public services are efficient, and the cost of living is relatively low compared to other European countries.

Culture and Leisure

From historic old towns to vibrant festivals and a lively café culture, Lithuania has something to offer for everyone. Nature lovers will enjoy countless forests, lakes, and parks.

Public Transport

Cities are well connected with affordable buses and trolleybuses, and intercity trains and buses are reliable and easy to use for traveling across Lithuania.
