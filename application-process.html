How to apply for studies in Lithuania

← Back to Studies

Main Admission Routes

International students usually apply via:

  • Apply.studies.lt – Centralized online application system
  • Direct university applications via their official websites

Key Requirements

  • Completed secondary education or equivalent
  • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL or equivalent)
  • Motivation letter, CV, and recommendation letters (for Master’s studies)

Deadlines

Application deadlines vary by university, but usually fall between **March and July** for autumn intake.
