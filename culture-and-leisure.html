Events & Festivals

From jazz festivals in Klaipėda to the Vilnius International Film Festival, Lithuania offers a vibrant calendar of cultural events throughout the year.

  • Vilnius International Film Festival (Kino Pavasaris) – Held every spring, showcasing local and global cinema.
  • Klaipėda Sea Festival – A summer event by the Baltic coast combining concerts, parades, and sailing shows.
  • Christopher Summer Festival – Classical and experimental music in unique venues like churches and courtyards.
  • Druskininkai Poetic Fall – A literary festival featuring poets from around the world.

Museums and Theatres

  • National Museum of Lithuania – Dive into the country's history and heritage.
  • MO Museum – Modern art museum in Vilnius with interactive exhibitions and events.
  • Lithuanian National Drama Theatre – Hosts contemporary and classic Lithuanian and international plays.
  • Lithuanian National Opera and Ballet Theatre – World-class productions of opera and ballet.

Nightlife and Cafés

Vilnius and Kaunas are famous for their charming Old Town districts full of:

  • Cozy cafés (e.g., Habits, Backstage Café, Taste Map)
  • Craft beer bars and breweries (e.g., Sakiškių Alus, Volfas Engelman Brewery)
  • Live music venues (e.g., Tamsta Club, Lizdas)
  • Nightclubs and rooftop lounges open until early morning

Nature & Outdoor Leisure

Lithuania is known for its lush nature, clean lakes, and accessible green spaces:

  • Take a weekend trip to Trakai Castle or kayak on its surrounding lakes
  • Explore the Curonian Spit, a UNESCO World Heritage site with dunes and beaches
  • Cycle or hike through Žemaitija or Aukštaitija National Parks
  • In winter – enjoy skiing or snowboarding in Druskininkai Snow Arena

Sports and Fitness

Lithuanians love basketball – it's the national sport. But you'll also find:

  • Affordable gyms and fitness studios
  • Indoor climbing, swimming, yoga and dance classes
  • Public outdoor sports parks and running tracks in most cities
  • Opportunities to join local sports clubs or events (marathons, bike rides)
