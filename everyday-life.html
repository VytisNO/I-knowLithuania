Shopping

Major supermarket chains include Maxima, Lidl, Iki, and Rimi. Shopping centers such as Akropolis and Ozas offer fashion, electronics, and food courts. Most shops are open from 8:00 to 22:00, including weekends.

Healthcare

Public healthcare is accessible with insurance. Private clinics offer faster service, often with English-speaking staff. Use eSveikata to manage appointments and health records.

Banking & Payments

Opening a local bank account is easy. Swedbank, SEB, Luminor, and Revolut are widely used. Contactless and mobile payments are common across shops and services.

Telecom & Internet

Lithuania has fast and reliable internet. Providers like Telia, Bitė, and Tele2 offer affordable packages. Prepaid SIM cards are available in most convenience stores.

Weather and Seasons

Four seasons: cold snowy winters, warm sunny summers, and mild spring/autumn. Be ready for quick weather changes.

Children & Education

Public kindergartens and schools are available in all cities and towns. Private and international schools are also an option, especially in Vilnius and Kaunas. Many playgrounds and child-friendly events make Lithuania welcoming for families.

Leisure & Recreation

Cities offer green spaces like Vingis Park in Vilnius or Ąžuolynas in Kaunas. Libraries, cultural centers, gyms, and sports clubs are easily accessible. Many outdoor activities, including cycling and nature trails, are popular with locals.
