Shopping

Major supermarket chains include Maxima, Lidl, Iki, and Rimi. Shopping centers such as Akropolis and Ozas offer fashion, electronics, and food courts.

Healthcare

Public healthcare is accessible with insurance. Private clinics offer faster service, often with English-speaking staff.

Weather and Seasons

Lithuania experiences four seasons: cold snowy winters and warm summers. Dress accordingly and prepare for changeable spring/autumn weather.
