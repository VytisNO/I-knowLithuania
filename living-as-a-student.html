Living as a Student

Affordable and vibrant student life in Lithuania

← Back to Studies

Living Costs

Students typically spend between €400–€700 per month on living expenses including rent, food, transport, and leisure.

Accommodation

  • University dormitories – affordable and social (from €70/month)
  • Private rental – rooms or apartments (from €200–€400/month)

Transport & Discounts

Students receive discounts on public transport, museums, cinemas, and more with an International Student Identity Card (ISIC).
