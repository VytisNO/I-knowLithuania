Living as a Student

Affordable and vibrant student life in Lithuania

← Back to Studies

Living Costs

Students typically spend between €400–€700 per month, depending on city and lifestyle. This includes:

  • Accommodation: €70–€400
  • Food & groceries: €100–€200
  • Transport: €6–€15 with a student discount
  • Leisure: €50–€100 (movies, events, gym, etc.)

Accommodation

Students have two main options:

  • University Dormitories: Budget-friendly and often include utilities. Prices start around €70–€120/month. Great for socializing.
  • Private Rentals: Rooms in shared apartments start from €200/month, while full apartments may cost €350–€600/month depending on location.

Popular platforms: Aruodas.lt, Domoplius.lt

Transport & Discounts

Public transport in major cities is reliable and cheap. Students with ISIC cards get up to 80% discounts.

  • Monthly transport pass (student): €6–€8
  • Bicycles and scooters are popular in spring and summer
  • Train and intercity bus discounts available for students

Useful: Vilnius Transport, Train Tickets

Student Life

Universities in Lithuania offer various clubs, events, and organizations where students can meet new people and get involved:

  • ESN Lithuania – Erasmus Student Network
  • University sports and hobby clubs
  • Student representation and volunteering

Most cities have a vibrant nightlife with bars, cafes, and music venues offering student discounts.

Food & Eating Out

Dining out is affordable:

  • University canteens: meals from €2.50–€5
  • Local restaurants: €6–€12 per meal
  • Supermarkets: Maxima, Lidl, Rimi

Vegetarian and vegan options are widely available, especially in bigger cities.

Health & Insurance

Students from EU countries can use the European Health Insurance Card (EHIC). Others should purchase private insurance or get state insurance if eligible.

  • Student Health Centers are available in most universities
  • Pharmacies are widely available and speak English
