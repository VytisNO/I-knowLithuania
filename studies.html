Universities and Programs

Lithuania has top universities like Vilnius University, VGTU, and KTU offering English-taught degrees in various fields. Many programs are internationally accredited and provide opportunities for research and internships.

Application Process

International students can apply through LAMA BPO or directly to universities. Required documents include a high school diploma or bachelor's degree, proof of English proficiency, and a motivation letter.

Living as a Student

Student life in Lithuania is vibrant and affordable. Monthly living costs are lower than in Western Europe. Students benefit from discounted public transport, dormitories, student cafés, and cultural events.
